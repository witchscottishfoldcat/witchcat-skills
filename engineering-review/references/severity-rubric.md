# Severity Rubric

Use consequence, not wording intensity, to assign severity.

- High: can corrupt data, break security boundaries, cause major outage, or invalidate core business behavior
- Medium: causes wrong behavior in common or important flows, but with bounded blast radius
- Low: maintainability, edge-case correctness, or observability issue with limited immediate impact

If unsure between two levels, ask which consequence is more likely in real use and choose based on blast radius.
